Welcome to Eevil Chess

Eevil Chess is an interesting spin on an old time classic. In Eevil Chess, either RNGesus can be on your side or be against you.
RNGesus determines an eevil number for you, which determines how difficult of a time you are going to have.
From vanishing pieces to shrinking board size, a player can have the time of his life or live an absolute nightmare from RNGesus.
